FAQ

Common questions.

How is this different from ChatGPT or a regular chatbot? +

Generic chatbots give generic answers. Our bots are trained specifically on your products, services, pricing, and brand voice. They know your business as well as your best salesperson — and they never sleep.

What do I need to provide? +

Your website URL, any product docs or FAQs you have, and a 30-minute call so we can learn your brand voice. We handle everything else — training, building, deploying.

Will it actually match my brand's tone of voice? +

Yes. We analyze your existing communications — website copy, emails, social media — to build voice guidelines. During testing, you tell us what sounds right and what doesn't, and we refine until it's perfect.

How much does it cost to run monthly? +

Hosting and AI API costs are typically $20-50/month depending on traffic. For most small businesses, it's under $30. There are no monthly fees from us — you pay once to build.

Can I update the chatbot's knowledge later? +

Absolutely. New products, updated pricing, seasonal promotions — we can update the knowledge base anytime. Clients on Professional and Enterprise plans get this included in their support period.

How do I install it on my website? +

One line of code in your website's footer. If you can paste text, you can install it. We'll walk you through it — takes about 2 minutes.
